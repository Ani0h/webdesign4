HOW TO PLAY

Two teams of five players go against each other in real-time to try to achieve victory. Taking down the opposing base is the main goal to achieve victory. teamwork and strategy is needed to accomplish this. There are over 110 heroes in MLBB, each of which have different abilities. The heroes are split into six categories or roles. These are: Assassin, fighter, mage, marksman, support and Tank. A balanced team is essential for victory.
Each player must choose a hero/Champion to play and a role to fulfil.





BATTLEGROUND

Lanes

There are three lanes that lead to the opposing base. The gold lane (top), EXP lane (bottom), and the mid lane. Between each lane is the jungle. Each lane has three opposing turrets. These turrets shoot at champions and deal a great deal damage.

Minions

Minions can draw the turrets attack leaving a hero free to attack it.

Creeps

Creeps live in the jungle. When killed, different creeps give different buffs.

Turtle

The Turtle will reward all teammates with Gold and EXP when defeated. So, rally all your teammates to take it down.

Lord

Once defeated, Lord will respawn from the base and fight alongside the team that defeated him. Lord can enflict enourmous damage to a base if left uncontested!
